FAQ's

About Dubai Real Estate

Is it a good time to buy property in Dubai?

Yes, Dubai offers high rental yields, zero property tax, and growing demand driven by global investor confidence and visa incentives.

How can I trust a real estate agent in Dubai?

Always work with a RERA-licensed agent from an established brokerage like Indus Real Estate. Our team is certified, regulated, and transparent in all dealings.

What are the typical buying costs in Dubai?

Expect ~4–7% in transaction costs (including DLD fees and commissions), depending on the property and project

Does Indus help with off-plan and ready-to-move properties?

Absolutely. We offer full support from developer launches to resale, rental, and even post-handover management.

What areas are best for investment?

This depends on your budget and goals—popular investment zones include Downtown, Business Bay, Dubai Creek Harbour, JVC, Dubai Maritime City, Dubai Islands, Dubai South, and Dubai Hills Estate.
